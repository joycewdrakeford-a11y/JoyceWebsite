FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about my book marketing services.

How long does it take to see results from your marketing strategies?

Most authors see improvements in visibility within 2 to 4 weeks of implementing the strategies. However, sustainable sales momentum typically builds over 2 to 3 months as algorithms recognize improved performance.

Do you work with all book genres?

Yes! I have successfully worked with authors across virtually every genre including Romance, Thriller, Mystery, Science Fiction, Fantasy, Self-Help, Business, Memoir, Children's books, and more.

I am self-published. Can you still help me?

Absolutely! In fact, the majority of my clients (approximately 80%) are self-published authors. Self-publishing gives you more control, which means my services can have an even greater impact.

What if I have already launched my book? Is it too late?

It is never too late! Some of my biggest success stories are book revivals where authors who launched months or even years ago saw dramatic improvements after implementing strategic marketing.

Do you guarantee bestseller status or specific sales numbers?

Honestly, no, and you should be wary of anyone who does. What I DO guarantee is a comprehensive, data-driven strategy tailored to your book, proven tactics, and measurable improvements in visibility.

What is your response time for inquiries?

I strive to respond to all emails within 24 to 48 business hours (Monday through Friday). For urgent matters, please mark your email as URGENT in the subject line.
